DIRECTIONS (public transit)

- From FIUMICINO "Leonardo Da Vinci" AIRPORT

Take the FS train to "Fara Sabina" for 7 stops.
Get off at Stazione Trastevere.
Then you can either walk home (20 mins) or take the tram 3B or H for a few stops.
The stop name you need to get off at is "Trastevere/Min. Istruzione".

- From CIAMPINO AIRPORT

Take the TERRAVISION BUS to TERMINI.
Hop on the bus H to "Dei Capasso" and get off at "Trastevere/Min. Istruzione".
Then walk for 1 min.

- From TIBURTINA FS

Take the train to "Fiumicino Airport" for 3 stops.
Get off at "Stazione Trastevere".
Then you can either walk home (20 mins) or take the tram 3B or H for a few stops.
The stop name you need to get off at is "Trastevere/Min. Istruzione".
Then walk for 1 min.

- From TERMINI

Take either the bus H or the N8 towards "Dei Capasso" for about 29mins.
Get off at "Trastevere/Min. Istruzione".
